Introduction and Concept

The name 'Pasha' in Pasha Token is inspired by my cat.

The purpose of Pasha Token is to turn our love for cats into a culture. Each of our cats is our own 'Pasha'.

Pasha Token aims to create and develop a new 'Pasha' culture together with the community. Our goal is to strive to be as much 'Pashket' as possible. (Pashket means having a respectable role in our community and is a title that can only be used by those who own a specific number of tokens. They are the pioneers of the community.)
